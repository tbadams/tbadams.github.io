Bio

I am a graduate of the Interdisciplinary Computer Science graduate program at Mills College with particular experience in Android development. I am also interested in databases, User Experience and web design. I additionally have a background in classical and electronic music and am particularly passionate about the intersection of music and technology.

Thesis

For my graduate thesis, I contributed to the open source project App Inventor for Android by designing and implementing a component enabling the creation of music-making applications. The project required knowledge of the Supercollider synthesis library, Android SDK, Android Native Development Kit (NDK) and Java Native Interface (JNI). I also designed and implemented scalability improving functionality that loads native libraries and assets based on application needs.

The first part of my change has been accepted, and the second is being submitted for review.
